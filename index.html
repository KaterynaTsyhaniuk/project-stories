ABOUT THE GAME

Step into the heart of Paris and join Ambre in Dazzly Stories: Ambre’s Secret, a mesmerizing narrative gem-matching adventure

Explore forgotten rooms, restore heirloom jewelry, and reveal a story of legacy, gemstones, and the fate of a magical bloodline

When Ambre’s Aunt Céleste mysteriously vanishes, Ambre inherits an old mansion — and a choice. Should she sell it to an insistent stranger or uncover the secrets Céleste left behind?

With only two days and help from childhood friends — a talking magpie and a mischievous cat — Ambre begins a magical journey to reveal her family’s hidden history

KEY FEATURES

  • Narrative Puzzle Adventure:

    Experience an enchanting story with magical choices and emotional depth

  • Parisian Mystery:

    Navigate beautifully illustrated scenes in a vintage Paris setting filled with clues and secrets

  • Jewelry Restoration Mini-Games:

    Match gems to repair historic crowns, rings, and royal artifacts

  • Learn Through Play:

    Discover real facts about gemstones and their lore while solving puzzles

  • Charming Companions:

  • Relaxing Gameplay:

  • Free to Play:

    Accessible to all with optional cosmetic upgrades and boosters

FAQ

  • Is the game story-driven?

    Yes. Your decisions shape the outcome — replay to uncover both endings.
